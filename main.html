Paris, France


Paris, the capital city of France, is known for its iconic landmarks, rich culture, and exquisite cuisine. It is a romantic and historic destination that attracts millions of visitors every year.

Top 5 Attractions

  1. The Eiffel Tower – The most famous symbol of Paris and an architectural marvel.
  2. The Louvre Museum – Home to the legendary Mona Lisa painting and thousands of artworks.
  3. Notre-Dame Cathedral – A masterpiece of Gothic architecture with stunning details.
  4. Montmartre and Sacré-Cœur – A historic district with beautiful views and artistic vibes.
  5. Champs-Élysées – A prestigious avenue known for luxury shopping and cafes.

Must-Try Foods

  • French Croissants – Freshly baked buttery goodness.
  • Baguette – A classic French bread with a crispy crust.
  • Escargot – A delicacy of cooked snails with garlic butter.
  • Crepes – Thin pancakes, both sweet and savory.
  • Macarons – Colorful meringue-based confections.
